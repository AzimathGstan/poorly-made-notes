# Signal
- Continuous-time signal $x(t)$
- Discrete-time signal $x[n]$ 
- Even signal $x(t) = x(-t)$
- Odd signal $x(t) = -x(t)$
- Even-Odd decomposition $x(t)=x_e(t)+x_o(t)$
Where $x_e$ is even part, $x_o$ is odd part, then 
$$
\begin{cases} 
x_e(t) = \frac{1}{2}[x(t)+x(-t)] \\ 
x_o(t) = \frac{1}{2}[x(t)-x(-t)] 
\end{cases} 
$$
